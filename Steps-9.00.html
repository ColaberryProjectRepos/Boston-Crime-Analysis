Create all Measures for Boston Crime Data (37 measures)

Click on the  at the top of the question to download the zip file containing the word document that contains all the DAX expressions for the 37 measures that will be needed to create these dashboards.


Step 1: I will demonstrate below how you can create one of these measures. You will then need to create the other 36 measures by copying them from the word document and pasting them into your dashboard. Some of them are pretty long due to being formatted correctly. Also the last 4 measures that begin with Make do not have a period in front of them but they are measures just the same.


You will see that some measures will have errors until all measures are completed. This is because some of the measures rely on other measures.



Want more help in creating measures, check out the quick measure section also. It was a huge help to me just beginning.
